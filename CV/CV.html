About me

I was born not so long ago, and since I was born I've had this passion for the web. I like developing all day long, and the more the web expands, the happier I am.

One of the things I like most of web developing is bug hunting. I find strange that many people don't like bug hunting, and on the contrary they try to avoid them at all costs. Bug hunting is so rewarding and I'm so happy when i find a big juicy bug in my .net. I try to develop carefully in order to have the biggest possible bugs, but not big enough to destroy all my hard work.

The other day a friend of mine told me that I could get paid to hunt bugs. I found it could be a great opportunity to hunt bugs as I love doing all day long, while also getting some money that I could spend to buy even more bugs at the fishing gear shop.

I hope you'll find my application interesting.
